About Me

My name is Ava Mazzella, I grew up in the West Village surrounded by arts from the moment I could be.
I was drawn to creation and performance as a means to externally process the way I see the world. I make mostly paintings now, but that's very subject to change.
I live with my parents Jay and Jessica, + my cat, Mr. Whiskers.
the pic attached is from a photography program i did.. you'll hear more later

I mostly work in acrylic paints, on canvas. I'm drawn to mixed abstract + portraiture/figure painting, and I use bright or heavily contrasting colors. I paint about social relationships, discomfort, and experiences. I also frequently include animals, such as fish or birds in many of my paintings, but that's lost on even me.
